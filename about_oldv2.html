About Astra SpaceX

Leading the future of aerospace materials

Our Company

Astraspacex is a specialized supplier of precision precious metals engineered specifically for aerospace and space applications. Operating seamlessly across India and the United States, we serve manufacturers of commercial aircraft, military defense systems, satellite platforms, and space exploration vehicles.

Our company combines deep technical expertise in materials science with understanding of aerospace manufacturing requirements. We work exclusively with materials certified for flight-critical applications, ensuring every transaction supports the highest safety and performance standards.

Our Mission

To supply mission-critical precious metals enabling safe, reliable aerospace and space exploration via highest quality, traceability, and technical support.

Our Vision

To be the preferred global supplier of aerospace-grade precious metals, recognized for technical excellence, certifications, and commitment to advancing flight and space exploration

Safety First

Every transaction is governed by aerospace safety principles with comprehensive quality assurance and rigorous testing protocols.

Technical Excellence

Our team of aerospace materials specialists ensures superior knowledge of material properties and compatibility with aerospace applications.

Reliability

Consistent on-time delivery, guaranteed material specifications, and dedicated technical support define our relationships.

Global Responsibility

Compliance with international aerospace standards, defense export controls, and ethical sourcing practices.

Why Choose Astra SpaceX

  • Aerospace Certification: All materials meet FAA, EASA, and international standards
  • Technical Excellence: Certified metallurgists in aerospace materials science
  • Quality Assurance: 100% traceability and batch testing
  • Secure Supply Chain: Defense regulations and export control compliant
  • Global Sourcing: Premium materials from certified suppliers worldwide
  • Mission Success: Trusted partner for critical aerospace programs
  • 24/7 Support: India and USA operations for round-the-clock assistance
